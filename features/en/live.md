# Live TV

> Watch and record live TV channels from around the world.
> Multi-platform, directly in your browser.

---

## What is it for?

Want to follow news, sports or live events:

- **Watch** channels from around the world
- **Record** a live stream to watch later
- **Translate** subtitles in real time
- **Monitor multiple streams** simultaneously

## How does it work?

**1. Browse** available channels from the Live page

**2. Click** a channel to start watching

**3. Watch live** — video plays in your browser

**4. Enable** translated subtitles if available

> Channels are detected and updated automatically.

## What you can do

- **Multi-window**: watch several channels at once
- **Recording**: save a live stream for later
- **Translation**: subtitle live streams
- **Notifications**: get alerts when a channel goes live
