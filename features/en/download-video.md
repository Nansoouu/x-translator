# Download a video

> Download any video from YouTube, X, TikTok, Instagram…
> Free, no account required.

---

## What is it for?

You found a video and you want to:

- **Watch it offline** (on a plane, on the subway…)
- **Translate it** into another language with subtitles
- **Edit it** in your favorite video software
- **Share it** on another platform
- **Extract the audio** for a podcast or a quote

## How does it work?

**1. Copy the link** from your browser

**2. Paste it** on the home page (main input box)

**3. Select "Download"** — we handle the rest

**4. Get your file** — video delivered in high quality

> The process takes a few seconds to a minute depending on length and source.

## Supported platforms

YouTube · X (Twitter) · TikTok · Instagram · Facebook · LinkedIn · Twitch · Vimeo · Dailymotion · Reddit

## What you can do next

- [**Translate the video**](translate-video.md) — add subtitles in your language of choice
- [**Edit it in the Studio**](studio.md) — add watermark, text, overlays
- **Export it** as MP4, with or without burned-in subtitles

## Limitations

- Videos up to 4K (depending on the source)
- 1.5h of free transcription per day
- Personal and private use only (see platform terms)
