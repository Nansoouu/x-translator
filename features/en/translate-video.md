# Translate a video

> Subtitle and translate any video in 20+ languages.
> The original video stays untouched — subtitles are added on top.

---

## What is it for?

You have a video in a language you don't master, or you want to reach a global audience:

- **Understand** a conference, tutorial or interview in a foreign language
- **Share** your content with an international audience
- **Learn a language** with subtitles in your native language
- **Make accessible** your videos for deaf or hard-of-hearing viewers

## How does it work?

**1. Paste the link** (YouTube, X, TikTok…)

**2. Choose the source and target languages**

**3. Confirm** — processing is fully automatic

**4. Watch** the video with synced subtitles

> The process takes about 2 minutes for a standard video.  
> Subtitles are synced frame by frame — no lag.

## Available languages

Français · English · Español · Deutsch · Italiano · Português · Nederlands · Polski · Русский · Українська · العربية · فارسی · עברית · हिन्दी · 中文 · 日本語 · 한국어 · Tiếng Việt · Bahasa Indonesia · Türkçe

## Subtitle formats

- **WebVTT** — readable in video players, editable
- **SRT** — standard editing format
- **Burned-in** — embed subtitles directly into the video
